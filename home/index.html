Services

We gave main three services from our customer.They are Delivery Service,Online Application Service and Renting Service

Delivery Service

When a customer request a deliver stock manager can view it. After checking it according to the destination Stock manager decide to confirm or reject the delivery request.If it confirm customer can get delivery service and If it rejected customer get a SMS.

Online Application

There is an important service from our customer online application service.Customer can view our product fill online application and purchase order item easily use internet.

Renting

If a customer requests a rent. Manager get a notification from the system. Then the manager send a quotation to the stock manager. Stock manager checks the availability of those items and dates and accept or reject.If he accept the request customer can rent machine spare part. If he rejects the rent request customer get a message to his mobile.
